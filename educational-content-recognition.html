Educational Content Recognition

Powered by our education-focused Optical Character Recognition (OCR) algorithm, textual, graphic, and numeric formulae contents, as raster images on paper, are scanned and procesed into editable format via layout segmentation.

Advanced Features

All Types of Mathematical Equations Supported

Our content recognition engine supports all types of mathematical equations, no matter how complex they are. The processed content will be output in LaTex, the academic standard typesetting system.

Diagram Understanding and Resoning

For geometric diagrams, our content recognition engine has the ability to automatically recognize geometric elements, like lines, circles, and points of intersections, and hightlight each component with colors and letters.
